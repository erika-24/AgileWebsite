CardioGuard Ring

Wearable ring for continuous cardiac monitoring. Tracks HR, SpO₂, rhythm, and blood pressure. 5 to 7 day battery, up to 30 days offline storage. Pairs with the CardioGuard app via Bluetooth for early detection and peace of mind.

  • 5-7 day battery life
  • 30 days offline storage
  • Continuous monitoring
$249 hardware
Usage instructions
  1. Charge the ring fully before first use.
  2. Install the CardioGuard app and create your account.
  3. In the app, go to Devices → Add device → Ring, then pair over Bluetooth.
  4. Wear the ring snugly on your index or middle finger for best sensor contact.
  5. Enable alerts and set your sensitivity with your caregiver or clinician.
  6. Review trends weekly and respond to High/Critical alerts promptly.
