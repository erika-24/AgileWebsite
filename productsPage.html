Featured Products

CardioGuard Ring

Wearable ring for continuous cardiac monitoring. Tracks HR, SpO₂, rhythm, and blood pressure. 5 to 7 day battery, up to 30 days offline storage. Pairs with the CardioGuard app via Bluetooth for early detection and peace of mind.

CardioGuard Band

Wrist wearable with continuous ECG and vitals. Syncs to the CardioGuard app over Bluetooth so you can monitor cardiac health without frequent hospital visits. Ideal for all-day wear when you prefer a band over a ring.

CardioGuard Patch

Single-lead ECG chest patch that sticks securely for days. Bluetooth connection to the app for continuous rhythm and event logging. Perfect for sleep or when you're not wearing the ring. Reduces gaps in monitoring.

CardioGuard Cuff

Smart blood pressure cuff that pairs with the CardioGuard app via Bluetooth. Logs BP alongside ring and band data for one view of trends. Helps with early detection and lower healthcare costs.

CardioGuard Clip

Finger pulse oximeter that syncs SpO₂ and HR to the app over Bluetooth. Use for spot checks or as backup to the ring. Lightweight and pocket-friendly for on-the-go cardiac peace of mind.

CardioGuard Hub

Bedside charger and Bluetooth sync station for your CardioGuard Ring. Overnight upload to the app and optional morning summary so you and your care team stay informed with minimal effort.
