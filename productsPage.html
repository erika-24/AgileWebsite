Blood Pressure

CardioGuard Cuff

Smart blood pressure cuff that pairs with the CardioGuard app via Bluetooth. Logs BP alongside ring and band data for one view of trends. Helps with early detection and lower healthcare costs.

  • Clinically validated accuracy
  • Stores 200+ readings
  • Guided measurements
$129 hardware
Usage instructions
  1. Pair: Devices → Add device → Cuff in the CardioGuard app.
  2. Sit relaxed for 5 minutes with feet flat and arm supported.
  3. Wrap the cuff on your upper arm (snug, tubing down the inner arm).
  4. Start a reading in the app and remain still while it inflates.
  5. Take 2 readings, 1 minute apart, and save the average.
  6. Measure at consistent times to improve trend quality.
